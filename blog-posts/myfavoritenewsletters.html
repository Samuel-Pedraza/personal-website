My Favorite Newsletters

By Samuel Pedraza

There are a lot of newsletters out there, and I tend to be a newsletter junkie, when it comes to tech. This is a collection of my favorite ones.

The Hustle:

The Hustle is probably one of my favorite newsletters. They talk a lot about different happenings in tech, and are really more a news aggregator, with a mild personal commentary. They link to long articles around the web, videos, and so forth.

Five Bullet Friday

Five Bullet Friday, by Tim Ferriss, is a brief look into what he is doing, reading, digesting, or listening, along with a popular social media post. Very fun, brief, and won't clutter up your inbox. This guy knows what he is doing.

Sidebar

Sidebar is a pretty popular design newsletter. It, like "The Hustle" is more of an aggregator, but less commentary. Just five simple links to around the web, with a brief description.
